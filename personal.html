Personal Life

Anime

I love reading manga and watching anime.

It has been a part of my life since the beginning and has surprisingly taught me alot.

I first started off watching pokemon on television as a small child and later found myself indulging in wider categories and genres of anime.

I even ended up reading the source material of many anime even though I didnt really enjoy reading at the time.

Animation

With my love of anime i have deveolped a passion for animation and animating as well.

What started off as a hobby of sketching frame by frame "doodles" in a flipbook ended up turning

into 3d movement animation using blender and other softwares.

Music

I love music and if not all, most people do as well.

I enjoy not only listening to music, but making it as well.

I started learning to play the saxophone in highschool and then went on to learn piano and bass as well.

I spend some of my free time getting better at the instruments I play and learning new ones on the way.
